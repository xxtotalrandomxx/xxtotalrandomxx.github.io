The Wrist

Pen, 2019

The Wrist is created using cross hatchings to fill in the shading of the bones. As I carefully drew the bones to their respectable sizes, it took much planning to fit them into the canvas.

I chose this because of the detailed cross hatching to depict how dark the shading should be.
