Portal to Another Reality

Acrylic, 2019

The Portal to Another Reality is portraying what seems like a wormhole that is visioning the future of the modern land. What was once a full bed of grass and nature, is then claimed by society and killing the plants and wildlife. This is made from acrylic paint.

I like the message behind this one as it is telling how society is demolishing the wildlife that nature had been growing for centuries before. It gives a sense of movement that tells people to be aware of the health of the world.
