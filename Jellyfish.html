Jellyfish

Adobe Illustrator, 2019

Jelllyfish is a composition art exercise that played around with many filters. It started out as a photograph of jellyfish, but was then diminished through a series of filters overlapping. It is very lightly seen as to what the subject is, although, it can be imagined that there are three jellyfish floating in the seabed.
