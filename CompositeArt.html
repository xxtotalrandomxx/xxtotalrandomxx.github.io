Closet of Memories

Adobe Illustrator, 2019

In the Closet of Memories, the concept was to choose a single setting idea between the group members and mine happened to choose storage places. Inside the closet are a bunch of older anime costumes and armory that is commonly know to the public, but shows that have ended leaving these memories of past characters that will give nostalgia to those who know the content.

I chose this piece because I liked how each piece of costumes feel like they are soulless. They feel like they haven't been touched in a while and have been stored away giving a nostalic ambiance.
