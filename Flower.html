Graffite (Still life), 2019

Drawn in my beginning art course, I learned how to draw reflection in objects and glass. This is a still life of a black jar, flower in vase, and the alcohol shaker. The main detail in this drawing is the lighting and shading that reflect from the surrounding objects..
