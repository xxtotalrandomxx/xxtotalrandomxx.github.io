The Walk

Digital Media Art (Video Art), 2020

The walk is about the daily journies that I would see constantly over and over again, so in this video, I decide to go and turn away from that same familiar path and instead, go off into a direction that seems to have freedom without a worry in sight.

The concept of this was to find where I wanted to be. In this, I show that I want freedom and peace as I look off into the sunset.
