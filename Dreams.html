Dreams

Digital Media Art (Video Art), 2020

Dreams is a continuation project from my other project, "Escape", that I used Adobe Aero, an augmented reality application, to make a video out of where I would consider myself "home."

The concept of this was to find where I wanted to be. In this, I show that I am dreaming or imagining of what I feel like I could have been when I am older in the future. Growing up, I had always wanted to be a professional in some sporting category.
