Reflection

Acrylic (Reflective Art), 2019

The Reflective Art I created in 2-D concepts started off as being an arrow that is pointing towards the center. The arrow is then replicated as reflections on every ninety degrees to create a painting that looks like a bigger picture. Not only is the figure based off of an arrow, it even looks like a star is made in the center and looks like bugs and critters in another perspective. The painting really plays with the mind to think more about it's hidden features.

I chose this acrylic painting I made because I enjoyed the concept of starting with one single minded object and expanding it by creating more of the same figure.
