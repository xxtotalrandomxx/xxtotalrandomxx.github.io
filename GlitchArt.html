Hatsune Noku

Digital Media Art (Glitch Art), 2019

Hatsune Noku is based off of a popular imaginary pop singer named, Hatsune Miku, but instead of seeing a smooth textured image, the whole image is distorted and seems as though it is a program glitching. The concept of this piece was to distort any image we chose through an audio application known as "Audacity." As seen, it is clear that the image is not proportionally correct as her arm is on the other side of the image and there are black glitch streaks going across the image. In a way, it is meant to give an unpleasant feel.
